## XAoC Corporation (f.k.a. Perun Dynamics)

XAoC Corporation began its life as Perun Dynamics, a low-profile entity buried deep within the layers of a Seychelles-based holding company. In 2049, Katerina Orlova successfully traced the company's true ownership to Bogdan Vasiliev, a retired FSB logistics colonel who had spent years operating in the shadows.

Recognizing the immense potential of Perun's early experimental tech, Katerina orchestrated a hostile takeover in 2050, at the age of thirty. Following the acquisition, she rebranded the entity as XAoC Corporation and leveraged her family's resources to scale the technology, eventually dominating the global prosthetics and neural interface market. By the 2070s, XAoC commanded approximately 78% of the prosthetic sector, projecting a public image of "precision for the human edge" while maintaining a ruthless, private operational infrastructure that continues to handle "anomalies" with the same efficiency Katerina once used to seize the company.
