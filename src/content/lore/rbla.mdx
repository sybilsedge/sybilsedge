**Real Body Liberation Army (RBLA)**  
*Anti-cybernetic terrorist network*  
**Status:** Dormant remnant cells  
**Founded:** 2035  
**Peak:** 2040s under Joseph Weber  
**Quote:** "Weaponized livestock will burn. You will rebuild from clean ash." [file:21][user]

**Core Ideology**  
Rejects all cybernetic prosthetics/neural interfaces as dehumanizing corruption. Views augmented humans as "weaponized livestock" requiring violent purification to restore organic humanity. [file:21][user]

**History**  
- **2035:** Small ideological group forms opposing cybernetic integration.  
- **2040:** Joseph Weber radicalizes leadership; global expansion, clinic bombings begin.  
- **2040s:** Political ties in East Asia; China intervenes after regional escalation.  
- **Post-2040s:** Organized collapse; small cells claim opportunistic credit. 

**Operations**  
Targeted cybernetic clinics, implant users, augmentation facilities via bombings. Real group; dormant but claims Alexander's false flags for relevance. 

**Current Threat**  
Fragmented cells (est. <20 active). Ideological persistence > operational capacity. 

**Visual Motifs**  
Charred clinics, organic-vs-mechanical posters, Weber surveillance photos.
