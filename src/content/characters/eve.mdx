## Overview

Eve, operating as Sister Margarethe, is the pragmatic fixer pursuing Alexander's downfall and trafficking dismantlement. Late-40s, she trades morally ambiguous deals (Phoenix Protocol) for justice, coordinating underworld/state networks.

Ruthless competence in gray world; calm control/moral certainty make her compelling support ally. 

## Profile

Cloistered Freiburg nun cover: steel eyes, efficient movement, analog books quirk in digital era. Former intelligence operative turned shadow mentor. 

Consistent pragmatic evolution: favors/tools for objectives, no major growth—decisive operative. 

## Appearance

Late-40s, cloistered habit/disguise, but sharp/efficient. Steel eyes betray operative past.

## Personality

Dry wit, mystique; calm under pressure, morally certain despite compromises. 

## Motivations

Dismantle sexual trafficking, justice vs. Alexander. Existential purpose via covert intel/deals. 

## Strengths and flaws

Strengths: underworld/state networks, decryption/intel coordination (Phoenix key).

Flaws: ambiguous methods cut deals compromising transparency/legality. 

## Relationships

Supports Katerina: Phoenix Protocol exchange, field assets/extractions. Anti-Alexander justice alignment.

## Character arc

Pragmatic fixer steady: trades for anti-trafficking wins (e.g., Istanbul setup).

## Themes

Gray-world justice, trafficking dismantlement, operative mystique. Methods vs. ends tension.
