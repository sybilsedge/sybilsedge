## Overview
Piotr Orlov is Katerina's older brother and logistics wizard—"hammer" to her blade. Ex-Nikita student turned Bay of Bankok smuggler, he bends routes for black ops supply chains. 

## Profile
Bar-fight tough: 5'11", 240lbs bull-necked tank. Titanium knee (Alexander arms run), cauliflower ear, knuckle scars ("Hammer polish"). Cargo-ring finger spin, room scans like manifests. 

## Appearance
Scarred bulk: Smuggler brawls, knee augment whirs under stress. Practical cargo pants/vests. 

## Personality
Gruff humor ("Routes like women: charm, don't force"). "Paths bend for hammer"—grit + flow. Impulsive but loyal. 

## Motivations
Prove "Orlov hammer unbreakable" (family redemption). Skeptical of Alexander evil tales. 

## Strengths and flaws

Flaws: Impulsive deals (grain scam loss), bar-fight escalations.
