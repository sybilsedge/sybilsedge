CEO of XAoC Cybernetics and ruthless architect of the company's global dominance in the cybernetic prosthetics market. Behind the polished executive façade, Katerina has spent decades waging a shadow war against her father, Alexander Orlova, whose criminal network quietly repurposed XAoC prosthetics as remote-detonated weapons.

When the first bomb detonates in St. Petersburg, Katerina knows exactly what it means — and exactly what it will cost her to stop the next one.
