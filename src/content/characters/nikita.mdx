## Overview
Nikita Rykov is the grizzled Spetsnaz vet turned Muay Thai/Krav Maga sensei—Katerina's "blade" mentor, dojo guardian, family tactical forge. Trains heirs in precision control, repaying dojo debt via unbreakable warriors. 

## Profile
58: Weathered Siberian oak—6'1", 220lbs wiry endurance. Knife scar, shrapnel forearms, no tattoos ("Ink for convicts"). Bamboo shinai taps (monk tic), prayer beads (Thai wat gift). 

## Appearance
Scarred survivor: Lean muscle, pockmarked forearms, cheek slash. Practical gi/track pants; beads necklace glints during drills. 

## Personality
Rigid code: "Form over fury"—breath/form > rage. Dry humor ("Muay Thai: your face meets my knee"). Spots "bad tells" instantly. 

## Motivations
Repay Katerina's dojo rebuild (Alexander arson '57). Forge warriors from heirs ("Blade serves sheath"). Thai monks healed heart—breathe past scars. 

## Strengths and flaws
Strengths: MA mastery (breath control), cynical scouting (terrain/monk reads), unbreakable loyalty. 

Flaws: Scarred detachment (ghosts ex-wife), rigid code breaks (knife pulls), pushes breaking point ("Pain forges"—lost pupils).
